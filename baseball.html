Baseball

I was a member of the Saint John's University baseball team for all 4 years that I attended college. I learned many lessons in leadership, time commitment and work-ethic with my teammates and coaches throughout our seasons. Growing up my coaches preached that while you may struggle hitting sometimes, defense can always be played with effort and urgency. I took this to heart and over my 4 years I formed a role as a defensive replacement in the outfield. I always did struggle hitting in college... (you can find my stats below) but I prided myself on catching every ball hit to the outfield and found my way to help our team succeed. I also play on the Twin Cities Saints men's amateur townball team in the summer.
